Music utility / iPhone

OrbitalDeck

OrbitalDeck is a focused iPhone companion that listens to nearby music, estimates its tempo, and sends MIDI Clock to connected performance devices. Manual tempo control is available when a fixed BPM is preferred. Microphone analysis happens on the device; the app does not record or upload microphone audio.
