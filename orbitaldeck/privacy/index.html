OrbitalDeck Privacy Policy

Effective July 16, 2026

Data collection

OrbitalDeck does not collect personal information, use advertising identifiers, or include third-party analytics or advertising services.

Microphone

With your permission, OrbitalDeck analyzes microphone input on your device to estimate tempo and beat timing. The app does not record, store, or upload microphone audio.

Bluetooth and MIDI

OrbitalDeck uses Bluetooth and MIDI to discover and communicate with devices you choose. Device profiles and app settings may be stored locally on your device. OrbitalDeck does not transmit this information to the developer.

Network services

The app does not require a developer-operated server. This policy page is hosted as a static GitHub Pages site. GitHub may process ordinary web request information under its own privacy terms when you visit this page.

Your control

You can deny microphone or Bluetooth access in iOS Settings and remove locally stored app data by deleting the app.

Changes

If OrbitalDeck's data practices change, this policy will be updated before the changed behavior is released.
