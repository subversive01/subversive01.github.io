Before contacting support

  • Confirm microphone and Bluetooth access are enabled for OrbitalDeck in iOS Settings.
  • Confirm the destination device is powered on, nearby, and advertising its MIDI service.
  • Include your iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.
